How we calculate Labor:
Labor estimates are dynamically scaled to industry standards based on structural complexity. Lightweight homes (Amakan/Metal) are estimated at 40% of material costs. Full 1-storey concrete homes require more masonry (45%), while 2-storey and loft structures require heavy formworks and concrete lifting (50%).
