How BuildWise Works

Our intelligent platform simplifies the complex process of construction estimation into four straightforward steps.

1

Select Budget

Start by determining your target budget bracket. This helps us tailor the available architectural models and material options to your financial scope.

2

Choose a Model

Browse our catalog of predefined architectural styles, ranging from modern lofts to classic two-storey homes, all engineered for optimal structural integrity.

3

Customize Design

Configure the specifics. Adjust areas, select your preferred materials, and define the finishes for the roof, walls, floors, and more through our dynamic builder.

4

Get the Estimate

Instantly generate a comprehensive summary. Review your total build area, a projected construction timeline, and a highly detailed bill of materials and costs.
