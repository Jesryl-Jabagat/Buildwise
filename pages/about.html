About BuildWise

The autonomous platform designed to simplify, optimize, and accurately estimate construction projects. BuildWise solves the complex challenge of architectural planning by combining automated structural models with real-time material and cost approximations.

Disclaimer

The formulas and methodologies utilized in BuildWise are based on universally accepted construction estimation principles and engineering references.

However, AI-generated estimates and material summaries are intended solely as preliminary planning tools. Environmental factors, soil conditions, local building codes, and material price fluctuations may significantly impact the final requirements.

Final architectural plans, structural computations, and project budgets should always be thoroughly reviewed, verified, and approved by a licensed professional engineer or architect prior to the commencement of any construction activities.
